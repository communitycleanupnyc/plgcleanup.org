# FAQ

The questions we hear most. Don't see yours? [Email us](mailto:communitycleanupplg@gmail.com)
or ask in the #general channel on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/H9her7r8N2).

## Can I earn community service hours?

Yes. We sign school, DOE, and National Honor Society forms on the spot at every
cleanup. See [community service hours in Brooklyn](/community-service-hours) for
how it works.

## Do I need to register?

No. Just show up — all supplies are provided. Find the [next cleanup](/join).
