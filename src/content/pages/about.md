# Our story

Community Cleanup PLG started in 2023, when a team of neighbors got tired of
stepping over the same litter on the same blocks and decided to do something
about it — together. What began as a few people with borrowed grabbers is now a
weekly volunteer trash cleanup crew in Prospect Lefferts Gardens, the Brooklyn
neighborhood at the heart of Flatbush and Little Caribbean.

The idea is simple, and it hasn't changed. Every Saturday morning we meet on a
chosen corner, hand out gloves, grabbers, and bags, and spend an hour making a
stretch of sidewalk visibly better. There's no registration and nothing to buy —
you just show up. Most cleanups draw five to ten neighbors; some draw many more.

Since we started, hundreds of volunteers have joined in, and together we've
pulled more than 3,000 pounds of trash off our streets — every cigarette butt
and takeout container counted. But the pounds are only part of it. The real
point is the block itself: cleaner, and cared for by the people who live on it.
