# Community service hours in Brooklyn

Need community service hours for school? Join one of our Prospect Lefferts Gardens cleanups in our Brooklyn neighborhood.

## How it works

1. Find the next cleanup on the [join page](/join): no registration, all
   supplies provided.
2. Bring your form (paper or a link to an online form) and show up.
3. Find the utility cart with trash grabbers, say hi to a squad leader, and let
   them know you need service hours signed.
4. At the end, the squad lead signs, dates, and stamps your form.

## Good to know

- Students and other learners all welcome.
- Bringing a class, team, or troop? [Email us](mailto:communitycleanupplg@gmail.com)
  ahead of time so we can plan to best accommodate you.
