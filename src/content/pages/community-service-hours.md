# Community service hours in Brooklyn

Need community service hours for school? Join a Community Cleanup PLG cleanup in
Prospect Lefferts Gardens, Brooklyn and earn **verified volunteer hours** for
work that helps your neighborhood.

We sign **school, DOE, National Honor Society, and court-ordered** community
service forms on the spot. A typical cleanup runs one hour; stay for as long as
you need and we'll confirm your time.

## How it works

1. Find the next cleanup on the [join page](/join) — no registration, all
   supplies provided.
2. Bring your form (paper or a link to an online form) and show up.
3. Find the shopping cart with trash grabbers, say hi to a squad leader, and let
   them know you need service hours signed.
4. At the end, an organizer signs, dates, and stamps your form.

## Good to know

- Students, scouts, and groups are all welcome.
- Bringing a class, team, or troop? [Email us](mailto:communitycleanupplg@gmail.com)
  ahead of time so we can plan for larger numbers.
- Every hour counts toward keeping Prospect Lefferts Gardens clean (and looks
  great on a college or job application :)
